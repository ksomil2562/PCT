One Person One Tree

The Paritosh Charitable Trust has launched a wonderful initiative called "One Person One Tree" to help the environment and make a positive impact in our communities. Here's how it works: if you want to help, all you have to do is donate some money to the trust. With that money, the trust will plant a tree. It's that simple! But here's the really cool part: when you donate, you can choose to plant a tree in honor of someone you love or to mark a special occasion, like a birthday or anniversary. So, not only are you helping the environment, but you're also making a meaningful gesture that celebrates the people and moments that matter to you. Imagine, every time you look at that tree, you'll remember the person you planted it for, or the special day you chose to commemorate. It's a beautiful way to give back to nature and spread joy at the same time. The goal of this initiative is to get as many people involved as possible. By planting one tree at a time, we can make a big difference together.
